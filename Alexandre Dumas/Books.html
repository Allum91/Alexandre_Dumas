Works


High Adventure

Alexandre Dumas wrote numerous stories and historical chronicles of high adventure. They included the following:

  • Captain Paul, 1838
  • Othon the Archer, 1840
  • The Fencing Master, 1840
  • The Corsican Brothers, 1844
  • The Black Tulip, 1850
  • The Mohicans of Paris, 1854


Fantasy

  • The Nutcracker, 1844 (a revision of Hoffmann's story 'The Nutcracker and the Mouse King')
  • The Pale Lady, 1849
  • The Wolf Leader, 1857 (One of the first werewolf novels ever written)

Monte Cristo

  • Georges, 1843
  • The Count of Monte Cristo, 1844-1846

The D'Artagnan Romances

  • The Three Musketeers, 1844
  • Twenty Years After, 1845
  • The Vicomte de Bragelonne, 1847
